What We Offer

SERVICES &
EXPERIENCES

🎮
Mobile Gaming Trailer

Step inside a 32-foot climate-controlled studio loaded with PS5, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, VR headsets, and 7 Arcade1Up machines — over 16,000 games ready to play at your event, rain or shine.

🍔
VS Burger Food Service

Fresh, made-to-order burgers, wings, Italian beef, walking tacos, and more — served straight from the VS Burger truck. Every order comes with fresh-cut fries. Add food service to any package.

📺
AOTC Mobile Billboard

Turn heads before guests even arrive. The AOTC LED display truck features 4 large screens, external audio, and custom video content — perfect for event signage, brand displays, and video backdrops.

🥽
VR Pop-Up Arena

Take the experience to the next level with an on-site virtual reality arena. Guests can step into immersive VR worlds — a guaranteed crowd-pleaser for all ages at birthdays, corporate events, and more.

🎥
Professional Videography

Every event is captured by a professional videographer. Select packages include a full highlight reel set to music — a cinematic keepsake your guests will share long after the event ends.

🏰
Bouncy House & Party Games

Keep every guest entertained with a 13x13 Crayon Castle bouncy house and a full suite of adult and family party games — included free in select packages and available as an add-on to any booking.
